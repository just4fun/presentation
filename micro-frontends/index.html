# Micro Frontends #### Zoro Jiang #### Aug 17 2020
## Definition
"An architectural style where independently deliverable frontend applications are composed into a greater whole" https://martinfowler.com/articles/micro-frontends.html
## History
1. The term first came up in [ThoughtWorks Technology Radar](https://www.thoughtworks.com/radar/techniques/micro-frontends) at the end of 2016
2. Not new concept. Extending the microservices idea to frontend development
## Evolution
![evolution](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/7512625/90331572-1cfd7200-dfe8-11ea-9fa8-27ac3e81ed6f.jpg) https://blog.bitsrc.io/state-of-micro-frontends-9c0c604ed13a
## Benefits
1. Simple, decoupled codebases
2. Different technologies (frameworks) are acceptable
3. Independent deployment
![independent-deployment](https://martinfowler.com/articles/micro-frontends/deployment.png) https://martinfowler.com/articles/micro-frontends.html
